About The Samaj

परम्परा जोगाउने, नयाँ पुस्तालाई जोड्ने।

यो वेबसाइट समाजको आधिकारिक डिजिटल केन्द्रको रूपमा डिजाइन गरिएको छ, जहाँ सदस्यता, प्रोफाइल, संस्था सूचना र नेतृत्व व्यवस्थापन सजिलो हुन्छ। Designed as the official digital center for membership, announcements, leadership coordination and community growth.

Member Self-Service / एकीकृत सदस्यता

Members can log in and update personal details, profession, district, business and bio.

Trusted Administration / संस्थागत विश्वसनीयता

Admins can approve new members, review profile updates and manage directory visibility.

Service & Opportunity / समाजसेवा र अवसर

Programs, support campaigns, education, health and business networking can be organized clearly.

Services / मुख्य सेवा

Useful tools for everyday community work.

समाजको सदस्यता, सूचना, कार्यक्रम र निर्देशिकालाई एउटै व्यवस्थित अनुभवमा ल्याउने modules। A complete foundation for membership, announcements, events and verified community records.

Member Directory

Search verified profiles, connect with community members, and view local chapter records.

News & Announcements

Stay informed on community events, notices, blood donation drives, and cultural programs.

Business Directory

Discover and support local entrepreneurs, services, and professionals in the community.

Membership Benefits

Create a verified account, join our family networks, and access portal social services.

Member Directory सदस्य निर्देशिका

A verified public identity for the community. समुदायको लागि प्रमाणित सार्वजनिक परिचय।

सदस्यको अनुमति अनुसार नाम, जिल्ला, पेशा र छोटो परिचय मात्र सार्वजनिक देखाउन सकिन्छ। Only approved profile fields appear publicly, based on member consent and admin review.

Events & Notices / कार्यक्रम

Active community, clear communication.

Admin ले सूचनाहरू, सभा, सामाजिक कार्यक्रम र अभियानहरू यही portal बाट publish गर्न सक्छन्। A clean communication space for meetings, programs, campaigns and important updates.

Annual General Meeting

Membership updates, reports and future planning discussion.

Youth Entrepreneur Meetup

Business, career guidance and networking session.

Community Service Campaign

Blood donation, health camps and student support programs.

Membership Application सदस्यता आवेदन

Start your digital membership. आफ्नो डिजिटल सदस्यता सुरु गर्नुहोस्।
